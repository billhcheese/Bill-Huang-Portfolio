Solar Folder

TLDR: Created a consumer solar panel system that is easy to install and affordable for capstone engineering degree

Topics

Machine Design, Deformable Bodies, Electronics, Manufacturing, Problem Investigation, Ideation, Innovation, MATLAB, CAD, SolidWorks

The video below summarizes the project pretty well. I was in charge of designing the actuation system that let the solar panel move and hold it's position. I created a script in MATLAB that would estimate the forces experienced by critical parts of the design under various load conditions from the wind and tilt angle of the solar folder. This was done to make sure the design would not fail. Machine design engineering principles were key to designing such a digital twin.

I love problem definition and solution ideation, I was heavily involved with and particular about those two things on the front end of this project. I used my previous project management expertise to make sure we were able to research, design, model, and manufacture this novel product to meet our deadlines.



The final product we built is shown below moving along its full range of motion. Due to budget constraints, we only built one side of the solar folder as this was a proof of concept.
